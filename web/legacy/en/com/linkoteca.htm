Links

Links to other sites and web pages related to the Clic project.

  • Material created for the Department of Education of the Balearic Islands (2011)
    Merce Oliver Oliver has created this collection of JClic activities designed to teach language to new students: Som i estam (comprehension and speaking) and Del món a les illes (reading and writing).
  • Biblioteca Elteumestre JClic d'Educació Infantil i Primària (2011)
    JClic llibrary with 1261 projects from clicZone in Spanish and Valencian-Catalan-Balearic languages. This library has been created by Manuel Fernández Segura. Updated on 2016.
  • Didattica - Software didattico per la scuola
    Italian portal devoted to good free educational software for primary school. Didattica has led the translation of JClic into Italian, and is an active contributor of the ClicZone projects library.
  • I Clic Meeting 2008
    Collection of papers and photographs about the Clic Meeting held in Castelló de la Plana on April, 5th, 2008.
  • Übungsprogramme für den Unterricht selbst erstellt ohne Programmierkenntnisse
    Pages of the German language speakers Clic community, held by Education Highway in Austria.
  • Le coin de clic
    Page of the French Clic community.
  • ClicApplic
    Educational website with Clic activities in French.
  • Actividades educativas con JClic
    Page with JClic activities in the compilation of educational resources done by Francisco Javier Rodríguez addressed to primary education.
  • Aplicaciones educativas
    Page with Clic activities in the compilation of educational resources done by Juan José Mateo Molina.
  • Argenclic
    Space created by Professor Fernando Pelillo with the aim of spreading the use of Clic in Argentina.
  • Maestroteca
    Web of educational links that distributes a compilation of Clic activities in CD-ROM organized by educational cycles.
  • AraClic
    Site of the Department of Education of the Government of Aragon.
  • MurciaClic
    Site of the Department of Education of the Murcia region.
  • Lapisdecores
    Lapisdecores of galego21.org is a site of resources in Galician language with a space dedicated to Clic activities.
  • WEIB
    Educational site of the Balearic Islands with educational resources made with Click.
  • CFA "Els tarongers"
    Page of a school for adults that offers different Clic activities in the resource section.
  • CD Clic Infocole Botanic Calduch
    Sample of the Clic activities used at the Botànic Calduch school.
  • ClicClub
    Page of the "Valle del Salor" CRA with activities done with Clic and a list of other pages with more activities.
  • CP San Lorenzo de Ezcaray
    This school in La Rioja offers  a compilation of about 50 activities classified by educational reason on its web page.
  • Recursos d'Educació Especial
    A lot of resources, documents and many Clic and JClic activities created by Margarita Fortuny. This site is in Catalan, but some of Margarita activities are also translated to other languages.

If you have or you know of other Clic pages and want to incorporate them in the list... send us a message!
