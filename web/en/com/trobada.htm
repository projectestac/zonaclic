Clic Meeting 2008

On April, 5th, 2008, the first world-wide meeting of the Click project’s users and friends will be held in Castelló. Book this date in your agenda, you can’t miss it!

The meeting is organized by the Seminar of Computer Science of Castelló, with the collaboration of the Jaume I University and the CEFIRE of Castelló.

We will punctually inform about the meeting details in this page and through the zonaClic's RSS channel as well.


Meeting aims

  • To directly meet some people who have contributed to make the Clic project become a reality along these years.
  • To think about the role of the "ICT" resources on the current school
    and how we would like to be in the future.
  • Share experiences and points of view around the world of Education.
  • To have a nice and enjoyable time!

Place and date

5th April of 2008 in the Jaume I University of Castelló (Spain) in the Escola Superior de Tecnologia i Ciències Experimentals
