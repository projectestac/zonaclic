Clic meeting 2008 - Social activities

The organising committee of the Clic Meeting 2008 has prepared some activities to have a good time together during and after the meeting. In the registration form check the activities, and transfer, before March 25th, the total amount of the activities chosen to the following bank account ES36-2072-1312-73-3100061393 stating the organization name "Seminari d'Informàtica de Castelló", your full name and your ID or passport number. Please include your name and your ID in the bank transfer details.

Lunch, Saturday April 5th in the University Jaume I

This lunch will take place at the University canteen and its price is 12 €. (To be confirmed).

Dinner, Saturday April 5th

The farewell dinner will take place at the Castelló Center Hotel. This is the menu:


Hake with baby squid sauce
"Agua de Valencia" sorbet
Sugary beef with rosemary and raisins

***
Hazelnut praline with coffee sauce
Water, coffee and spirits

***

White and red wine "Viñas del Vero (DO Somontano)"
Cava "Dominio de la Vega Brut"

The price for the dinner is 40 €.

Tour, Sunday April 6th

The last activity of the meeting is a tour around Castelló on April 6th. The tour will allow us to know the city and sorroundings of Castelló. We will visit "Mayor" square, "el Fadrí", the "City Hall", "Las Palmas" desert... Return to Castelló at 1:30 pm.

This trip is subsidized by the county council of Castelló. (To be confirmed).
