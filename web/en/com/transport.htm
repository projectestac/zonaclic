Train

Renfe (Spanish train company) main page: www.renfe.es

Possible itineraries:

  • From Madrid (Atocha Station) to Castelló.
  • From de Barcelona (Sants Station) to Castelló.
  • From Madrid (Atocha Station) to València (Nord station).
  • From Valencia to Castelló:
    Renfe suburban train from Valencia (estació de trens de Valencia Nord) to Castello and return.
    From Monday to Friday, every 15 min, from 6:00 am to 10:00 pm
    Saturday, every hour, from 6:00 am to 21:50 pm
    The journey lasts approximately 1 hour
    Valencian suburban train information : www.renfe.es/empresa/cercanias

Further information about prices and timetables:

  • http://horarios.renfe.es/hir/ingles.html

Cotxe

Private car:

  • From Madrid to Valencia, through the highway A-III. From Barcelona to Valencia, through the highway A-7 which connects north and south of Spain by the coast. The road N-340 also connects north and south of Spain by the coast, no toll.
  • Exit 46 Castello Nord or Exit 47 Castello Sud from the highway.
  • Exit C-238 Castello-Centre from the road N-340.
