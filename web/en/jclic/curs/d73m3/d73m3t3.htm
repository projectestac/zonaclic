Identification Activity

In the identification activities there is only one group of information. To solve the activity you have to click on the boxes which fulfil lthe condition specified in the message .This information can be text, sound, graphics, music, animation or a combination of different types.

When the answer is correct, the content of the box disappears or an alternative piece of information appears.
 

 

To edit an identification activity so as to indicate the boxes that reunite the specified condition click on the grid found in the tab Relations:
 
 
There can be as many boxes as you wish. Obviously, at least one box must have the right condition because if not, the activity would be impossible to solve. There can be as many boxes marked as you wish.

The use of the tab Panel is the same as for simple and complex associations.
