This course is structured so that each of the modules begins with a series of theoretical documents where the basis of what will be worked on in the practice sessions is set down. It is very important to read these documents before starting to work.

In this first module there is a general look at JClic and JClic author, and also the different types of activities from installing and configurating, to a deeper glimpse into common aspects of the different types of activities.
